Continuity

From the textbook White Space is Not Your Enemy:our minds will continue a pattern beyond its ending points. Further, our eyes will follow the direction of a line. Our minds see a single lines or continuation of lines. Applying this concept can add a sense of direction and movement to your layout. Ergo, flow.

The element of continuity allows elements of a design to appear to be extended for better flow. The eye is drawn along a path, line or curve, preferring to see a single continuous figure than separate lines. This can be used to point towards another element in the composition, and is seen where a line is cut through one object, often in a curve, aligning perfectly with a secondary element
