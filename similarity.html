Similarity

From the textbook White Space is Not Your Enemy: our minds group things with similar properties, such as color or shape. “ Like goes with like.” In this example, we group squares with squares and circles with circles. In layout, we can use similarity to create order and organization through unity.

Similarity occurs when objects look similar to one another. People often perceive them as a group or pattern.As mentioned in the unity section of this website unity is an essential part of design that allows the design to come together seamlessly. Similarity is one of the significant contributers to unity in a design.
